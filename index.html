    OUR HISTORY

    ReaperMC has been in Development for over 2 years now. What started off as a small community minecraft server has developed into something we couldn't even imagine.

    OUR STAFF

    The ReaperMC staff team are friendly, dedicated, and always online to help everything run smoothly. Have any questions? Make sure to do /helpop ingame to reach out!

    OUR SERVERS

    We have HCF, Practice, and SkyBlock on our network, all with fully custom builds and useful perks and features. Join today at ReaperH.CF!

    OUR GAMES

    SKYBLOCK

    Skyblock is a gamemode where you start on a floating island with minimal resources. Compete with others or join them and jump to the top of the leaderboards! Difficult challenges are set for you through your sky adventure, complete them for rewards! It takes perseverance, dedication, and effort to get to the top!

    HCF

    HCF is a popular team-based, pvp orientated gamemode where you claim land, build a base with your friends, and teamfight other factions

    Practice

    KitPvP is a competitive, fast-paced gamemode packed with players! Chose your kit and join the race to the top of the leaderboard. Explore our custom map and test your skills on parkour in secret rooms. Whether you are an archer, gladiator or tactician there is a kit for you. Join the fight today on MyServer!

    What is Grim

    Grim is our brand new Cheat Detection System. Grim gathers and analyses data of users and determines whether a person is cheating or not. Grim also has a ClientSide Cheat detection unlike Cheatbreaker where your forced to run there modpack and launcher Grim has a application that check your minecraft for any suspustion stuff and only check when it is run.
